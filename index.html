Synopsis

Two brothers on an overnight fishing trip encounter a macabre family that compels the brothers to fight for their survival in a Louisiana swamp.

Two brothers, Billy and Kenny, haven’t spent time together in a while and decide a weekend frog gigging trip is the perfect getaway for the two siblings to bond together as brothers. Billy the older brother works for the Louisiana Power and Light, he’s rugged and single…you could call him a man’s man. Kenny on the other hand is a soft-spoken Baptist preacher who is married with a wife named Lorraine expecting their first child. Lorraine is leery of Billy and is afraid he will get Kenny into trouble. Upon their arrival at Honey Swamp, the brother’s decided to have a drink at the local marina before they head out into the swamp at night to gig ‘frogs’…a real southern delicacy. The marina is a real red neck’s paradise with mismatched furniture and pool tables, drunks fighting and an old man brewing a large barrel that appears to be gumbo. The brothers quickly discover the patrons of the fishing marina exist way outside of human norms and soon become captives of a deadly family intent on committing unspeakable horrors. As conversation ensues it turns out that Billy, Kenny, and Twila a captured college student is going to become the main course for a birthday dinner for one of the relatives. Locked away in a tool shed as one of the relatives prepares the barbeque grill…the trio of Billy, Kenny, and Twila escape into the swamp. Billy is injured with a gunshot wound. Billy knows his way around the swamp but is quickly losing consciousness due to blood loss…leaving the trio in the hands of Kenny, an inexperienced fisherman who can’t even swim and is deadly afraid of snakes. The cannibals prowl the swamp looking for the trio. “Flesh Off the Grill” will take you on an incredible swamp journey inside the evil hearts of cold-blooded killers who view life as a grotesque normality and a preacher who learns that life hangs by a delicate thread. Read as the plot twists change with every bend in the swamp…hang on to a climax that will reel you out of your seat into the swamp world of “Flesh Off The Grill.”

Jimmy Drain


Jimmy Drain is an actor/writer/producer from Denver, Colorado. He began his acting career in Hollywood California under acting coach Stephen "The Pit Bull" Snyder and Mr. Leo Rossi. Trekking from Colorado and Hollywood has landed him starring roles in over 55 short films and 8 feature films including "The Goal" 2005, "Don't Call Me" 2007, and the award winning short films "Suicide For Two" 2005 & "Dreamlady" 2009, "Broken Cross" Starring horror legend Tony Todd and "Meet Michael" 2017. In 2006 he birthed ThunderKnight Entertainment LTD, which has several feature film projects in development including "The Dead Rose", "Men Mistaken For God", "Pale Dagger", "Less Than Human" and "The Initiation" a collegiate horror/drama starring Vernon Wells, Mel Novak, Lauren Mayhew and Naomi Grossman (American Horror Story) Directed by: Brian McCulley.

"The Initiation" will be ThunderKnight Entertainment's first full length feature film production and Drain's first leading man acting role in a full length feature - as he continues his pursuit to infiltrate the Hollywood system.
